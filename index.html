About Us

The Games Club is a open-to-public video games group that aspires to be a relaxing and fun place for people to enjoy. We aim to provide our members with a comfortable place to meet new friends and bond over weekly scheduled competitive and co-operative gaming sessions, as well as just being a nice place to hang out and chat.
Session times as well as general venue opening times can be found below, or head on over to our Tournaments page to sign up to a competitive video game tournament for a chance to win a prize!
